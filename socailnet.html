SocailNet

This is a social App using Firebase. Posts can be shared publicly or with pre-approved followers. Users can like photos and follow other users to add their content to a feed. Users can also chat messages in RealTime.

  • Category: Android Application

  • Github: https://github.com/hahpuc/SocialNetAndroid

  • Demo Video: https://drive.google.com/file/d/1t2_Kh_FfoL161quLSgxCvijdg0wMdk-Q/view?usp=sharing

  • Technologies:
  • Anroid Studio 4.1.1

  • Kotin 1.3.72

  • Google Firebase

  • Circle ImageView

  • Fetch image from URL by Picasso

  • Make complex RecyclerViews Easily with Groupie
